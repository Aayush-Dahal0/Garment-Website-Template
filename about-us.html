Our Story

Building Quality Garments Since 2014

Thecho Garments was founded in Thecho, Lalitpur with a simple mission — to produce world-class garments from Nepal that compete on quality, craftsmanship, and value in the global market.

Starting from a small workshop with just 10 skilled workers, we have grown into a full-scale manufacturing operation with modern machinery, trained professionals, and a reputation trusted by brands in over 15 countries.

Our strength lies in our people — experienced cutters, skilled seamstresses, quality inspectors, and a management team that understands what international buyers expect. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern production techniques to deliver garments that are built to last.

What Drives Us

Our Mission

To be Nepal's most reliable and quality-driven garment manufacturer, delivering products that meet international standards while empowering our local workforce and communities.
