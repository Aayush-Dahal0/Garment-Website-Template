About Us

Crafting Quality Garments from the Heart of Nepal

Nepal Garment Co. is a leading garment manufacturer based in Thecho, Nepal. We specialize in producing premium-quality garments for brands, retailers, and businesses around the world.

With a modern 10,000 sq ft production facility, skilled workforce, and deep commitment to quality, we deliver garments that meet international standards.
