We've Got Answers

Here are the most common questions from our buyers and partners. Can't find your answer? Feel free to contact us.

Our MOQ varies by product type. Generally, it starts from 100–300 pieces per style per color. For custom designs, we recommend 200+ pieces for the best pricing. We can discuss flexible quantities for first-time orders.
Yes! We encourage sample development before bulk production. We create samples based on your tech pack or design reference. Sample development typically takes 7–14 days. A nominal sample fee may apply, which is often adjusted against the bulk order.
After sample approval, bulk production typically takes 25–45 days depending on order size and complexity. We provide a detailed production timeline with your quotation and keep you updated throughout the process.
We accept bank wire transfer (T/T), with typical terms of 50% advance payment and 50% before shipment. For established clients, we can discuss Letter of Credit (L/C) terms. Payment terms are negotiable based on order size and relationship.
Absolutely! We specialize in private label and OEM manufacturing. We can produce garments with your brand labels, custom tags, packaging, and all branding elements. Visit our Custom Manufacturing page for details.
We currently export to 15+ countries including India, Japan, UK, Germany, USA, Canada, Australia, UAE, France, South Korea, and more. We handle all export documentation and can ship via sea freight, air cargo, or international courier.
We follow internationally recognized quality standards including AQL 2.5 inspection, 4-point fabric inspection, and multi-stage quality checkpoints. Our trained QC team inspects every garment before it leaves our facility. Visit our Quality & Export page for more.
Yes! We accept tech packs, design sketches, reference images, or even verbal descriptions. Our design team can also help refine your ideas and develop proper tech packs if needed. We're flexible and collaborative in our approach.
We offer multiple shipping options: sea freight (most economical for large orders), air cargo (faster delivery), and international courier services (DHL, FedEx, etc. for small shipments/samples). We handle customs clearance and provide full tracking information.
