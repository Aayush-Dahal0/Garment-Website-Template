Our Process

How Custom Orders Work

A clear, step-by-step process so you always know what to expect at every stage.

1

Share Your Requirements

Send us your tech packs, design sketches, reference images, or simply describe what you need. We'll review and provide a detailed quotation.

2

Sample Development

We create detailed samples for your approval. You can request revisions until the sample matches your exact specifications.

3

Fabric Sourcing & Cutting

We source high-quality fabrics from our trusted suppliers and begin precision cutting based on approved patterns and grading.

4

Production & Sewing

Full-scale production begins on our modern equipment. Each piece is sewn, finished, and checked by our skilled workers.

5

Quality Inspection

Multi-point quality checks at every stage — from inline inspection to final AQL inspection before packing.

6

Packing & Shipment

Custom labeling, poly-bagging, carton packing, and export documentation. Shipped to your door with tracking.

Key Details

What You Need to Know

Minimum Order Quantity

MOQ starts from 100–300 pieces per style/color depending on the garment type and complexity.

Lead Time

Sample: 7–14 days. Production: 25–45 days depending on order size. Rush orders considered.

Custom Labeling

Woven labels, printed tags, hang tags, care labels, custom poly bags — all available and included.
