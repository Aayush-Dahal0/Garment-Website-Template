Quality Assurance

Uncompromising Quality at Every Step

Quality isn't just a department — it's embedded in our culture. Every garment we produce goes through a rigorous multi-point quality inspection process from raw material to final packaging.

Our trained QC inspectors follow internationally recognized standards including AQL 2.5 sampling methods to ensure each shipment meets or exceeds your quality expectations.

Our QC Process

Quality Checkpoints

We inspect quality at every stage of production — not just at the end. Here's how we ensure consistency.

Fabric Inspection

  • 4-point fabric inspection system
  • Color fastness testing
  • Shrinkage testing
  • GSM and weight verification

Cutting QC

  • Pattern accuracy check
  • Shade sorting and matching
  • Marker efficiency verification
  • Cut component counting

Inline Inspection

  • Stitching quality check (SPI)
  • Measurement accuracy
  • Seam strength testing
  • Workmanship inspection

Final Inspection (AQL)

  • AQL 2.5 sampling inspection
  • Full measurement audit
  • Appearance and finishing check
  • Packing and labeling verification
